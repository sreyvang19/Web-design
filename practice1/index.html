Tohoku on alert as Tropical Storm Maria moves over region


A top-level emergency warning was issued in an Iwate Prefecture city as Tropical Storm Maria moved over the Tohoku region on Monday, with some residents urged to evacuate or take other measures to protect their lives. At 10:30 a.m., but the alert was lifted at 2:45 p.m. The alert was issued for a total of 4,177 households and 8,300 people in the areas.
